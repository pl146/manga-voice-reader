Manga Voice Reader Auto-reads manga speech bubbles aloud

How to use:

1. Go to any manga website
2. Click "Open Reader" above
3. Click "Read Page" in the panel
4. Highlights + audio play automatically

Works on:

  • MangaDex
  • Webtoon
  • MangaPlus
  • MangaKakalot
  • Any site with manga images
